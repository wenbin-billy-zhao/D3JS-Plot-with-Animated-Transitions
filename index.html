D3 JS in Motion

A Show Case of D3.js's Advanced Animation Transition Capabilities
Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

The above scatter plot shows the correlations between several factors of social, economical, and health indicators of any givien state.

By clicking on the labels of both X and Y axis, you can see the correlations dynamically updated through D3.js's transition engine.

This is a very effective visualisation technique to show data insights and intuitions.
